About

Kenzie uses recipies that have been passed throughout the family to create cookies as good as your grandmother made them. They are baked with fresh ingredients and love. Kenzie's Cookies will be your go-to place for special occasions, parties or just a cheat day. We sell baked cookies and cookie dough. We give free cookies to school children Monday through Friday from 3-4 p.m.We are also pet friendly!

Fundraising

Need a fundraising idea? We sell cookie dough containers in bulk. A certain percent of every cookie dough container sold will go towards your funraiser. Orders will be ready as soon as a week after being turned in. Call 555-8426 for more information. Stop in and visit Kenzie's Cookies yourself! You can find us at 300 W. Sweet Avenue.
